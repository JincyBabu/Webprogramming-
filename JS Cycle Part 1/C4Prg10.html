SJCET,Palai

St.Joseph Engineering and Technology(SJCET) is an Indian Private College managed by the Syro-Malabar Catholic Diocese of Pala,located in Pala,Kerala,India. It is an AICTE approved college offering Professional Degree courses.SJCET Palai is a constituent college of Mahatma Gandhi University,Kottayam and APJ Abdul Kalam Technical University
