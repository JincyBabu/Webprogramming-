Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is an ivory-white marble mausoleum on the southern bank at the river Yamuna in the Indian city of Agra.It was commissioned in 1632 by the Mughal emperor Shajahan to house the tomb of his wife,Mumtaz Mahal;It also houses the tomb of Shah Jahan himself. The Taj Mahal was designated as a UNESCO World heitage site in 1983 for being "The jewel of Muslim art" in India and one of the universally admired masterpiece.The tomb of is the centerpiece of a 17-hectare(42-acre)complex,which includes a mosque and a guest house,and is set in formal gardens bounded on three sides by a crenellated wall
