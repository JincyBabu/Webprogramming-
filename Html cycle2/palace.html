Mysore Palace

The Palace of Mysore is the most famous historical monument and the royal residence at Mysore in the Indian State of Karnataka,and one of the most visited monuments in India.Mysore Palace is one of the most famous tourist attractions in the city along with Chamundi Hills.Mysore is commonly described as the ;City of Palaces',and there are seven places including this one;however Mysore Palace refers specifically to this one within the old fort.It is the official residence of the Wadiyar dynasty and the seat of the Kingdom of Mysore.
